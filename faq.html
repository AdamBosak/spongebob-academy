General Questions

What is Spongebob Academy?

Spongebob Academy is the world's finest underwater institution dedicated to training future fast food professionals.

Who can enroll?

Anyone passionate about flipping patties, running a fast food empire, or learning the secrets of the Krabby Patty formula (just kidding, that's classified).
